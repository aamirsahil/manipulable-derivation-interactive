We have now gained some intuition about how the force, and its location, can affect the behavior of a string. But what is exact nature of this relationship ? In order to find that out, we need to introduce an important assumption in our derivation. We assume that our string is composed of numerous small mass points which are connected to each other by springs. The distance b/w any two mass points is dx, which tends to zero.
